DAY 1

Squats

How To:

Start with legs spead to shoulder width standing up straight. Then proceed to bend your knees while pushing your glutes back. Reach the lowest level possible then push yourslef back up
(4 sets 10 reps)

Bench Press

How To:

Lay down on the bench with a slight back arch. Push the bar up then pull it to your chest where it should touch the middle of your chest and then push it back up
(4 sets 10 reps)

Shoulder Press

How To:

Hold dumbbels an shoulder width making a 90 degree angle with your upper arm and push up untill both arms are parallel
(4 sets 10 reps)

Lat Pulldown

How To:

Make sure to have a straight back and wide shoulder. select a weight that is convinient and pull down and hold for a few seconds
(4 sets 10 reps)

DAY 2

Jump Rope

How To:

Pretty self explanatory. Hold each end of the rope in a hand and at shoulder height and rotate and jump before the rope hits your legs
(4 mins in 1 min interval)

Jumping Jacks

How To:

As you jump open your legs and arms in the air. then the next jump close them and keep alternating
(2min in 30 seconds intervals)

Push Ups

How To:

Pretty common. set your self up like the picture above. arms should be a bit wider than shoulder length and next to your chest level. push yourself up repeatedly
(3 sets 20 reps)

Plank

How To:

Pretty common. set your self up like the picture above. make sure you engage your core and hold
(1min aim for 2mins)

DAY 3

Tri Pushdown

How To:

Use the cable machine for this workout. Set it up for the highest level possible hold the bar to chest level and push down till you activate the triceps
(4 sets 10 reps)

Bi Curls

How To:

Use the cable machine for this workout. Set it up for the lowest level possible hold the bar to waist level and pull up till you activate the biceps. Make sure your hands reach the level of your shoulders.
(4 sets 10 reps)

Pull-Ups

How To:

Find a Bar in the gym similar to the one in the picture. hold on to both sides and engage your core so you wont swing.Pull yourself up and then back down while negaging your core
(4 sets 10 reps)

Running

How To:

Find a treadmill set a decent pace and run for a few mins. If you can't find a treadmill you can run anywhere
(10-20mins)
