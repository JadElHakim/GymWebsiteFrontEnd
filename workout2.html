DAY 1

Bench Press

How To:

Lay down on the bench with a slight back arch. Push the bar up then pull it to your chest where it should touch the middle of your chest and then push it back up
(4 sets 10 reps)

Cable fly

How To:

Set the cable machine to the highest level grab each end using the neutral grip. Straight back and pull both ends to the middle of your chest.
(3 sets 8-12 reps)

Tri Pushdown

How To:

Use the cable machine for this workout. Set it up for the highest level possible hold the bar to chest level and push down till you activate the triceps
(3 sets 8-12 reps)

Dips

How To:

Find two parallel bars in your gym. set yourself like the picture above and then go down and push yourself up while focusing on activating the triceps.
(4 sets 10 reps)

DAY 2

Lat Pulldown

How To:

Make sure to have a straight back and wide shoulder. select a weight that is convinient and pull down and hold for a few seconds
(3 sets 8-12 reps)

Face Pulls

How To:

set the cable machine to head level. Use light weight and attach the rope to the cable machine. grab both ends of the rope stand far away and pull the rope towards your face.
(4 sets 10 reps)

Bicep Curls

How To:

Hold the dumbbels prallalel to your body. Then pull the biceps towards your shoulders while rotating them 90 degrees to form a perependicular with your body. Focus on bicep activation.
(3 sets 8-12 reps)

Hammer Curls

How To:

Hold the dumbbels prallalel to your body. Then pull the biceps towards your shoulders while keeping the dumbbels parallel. (3 sets 8-12reps)

DAY 3

Squats

How To:

Start with legs spead to shoulder width standing up straight. Then proceed to bend your knees while pushing your glutes back. Reach the lowest level possible then push yourslef back up
(4 sets 10 reps)

Deadlift

How To:

set the barbel on the ground. legs shoulder width, grip at shoulder width or a bit wider as well. Straight back and pull the weight while keeping a straight back.
(3 sets 8-12 reps)

Shoulder Press

How To:

Hold dumbbels an shoulder width making a 90 degree angle with your upper arm and push up untill both arms are parallel
(3 sets 8-12 reps)

Military Press

How To:

Set the barbel on the squat rack, use light weight and hold the bar at shoulder width. push the bar up not away , meaning it goes over your head and back towards your shoulders.
(3 sets 8-12 reps)
